Dictée is an iPhone/iPad app for pupils learning French dictation.

Every week, teachers hand pupils a paper word list to memorise. There's no good digital tool to practise spelling from that list — pupils just reread it and hope for the best.

The app lets a pupil photograph the teacher's word list, then practise spelling every word. The app reads each word aloud in French, and the pupil responds — either by typing the answer or by writing on paper and photographing their answer sheet. No feedback mid-session; the whole list runs first, then a results screen shows the score. Exactly like a real dictation exam.

Words the pupil keeps getting wrong are saved to a Review Bank, and a special Revisit session drills only those words until the pupil finally gets them right.

Zero friction, works entirely offline, no accounts needed. Photo in → practising in under a minute.
